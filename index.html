About Pandora English School

At Pandora English we cater to learners of all abilities and walks of life. We offer both group and private lessons delivered via online instruction. All of our teachers are native English speakers, and have passed tests to ensure their language skills meet the highest standard.

It is our belief that English language instruction should be based around 4 main competencies: Reading, writing, speaking and listening. Our curriculum has it's foundations from the methods used at Cambridge English, and guarantees results. Whether your goal is simply to become proficient in English conversation, achieve top-end results on your IELTS, or get a promotion at work, Pandora English can help.

What we offer

Pandora offers instruction for pre-A1 learners, A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2 and business English learners, in the form of private and group lessons.

Competencies Achieved
This level of English is about introducing easily understood concepts to children, in a way that engages them and fosters their interest. A pre A1 student will learn to understand basic English content on the internet, enjoy books, songs, television and films in English, and make friends globally. A1 students will learn to understand basic instructions and taking part in simple conversations, as well as completing basic forms, and entering dates.
Competencies Achieved
An A2 student will learn to understand simple written English, communicate in familiar situations, use basic phrases and expressions, and interact with English speakers who talk slowly and clearly. A B1 student will read simple textbooks and articles in English, write letters and emails on everyday subjects, understand factual information, and show awareness of opinions and mood in spoken and written English.
Competencies Achieved
A B2 student will learn to communicate effectively face to face, express opinions and present arguments, write clear, detailed English, explaining the advantages and disadvantages of different points of view, follow the news and write letters, reports, stories and lots of other types of text. C1 students will learn how to communicate effectively at the university and professional levels, participate with confidence in workplace meetings, and express oneself with a high level of fluency.
Competencies Achieved
Advanced learners in English will be able to study demanding subjects at the highest level, including PhD programmes, negotiate and persuade effectively at senior management level in international business settings, understand the main ideas of complex pieces of writing, talk about complex or sensitive issues, and deal confidently with difficult questions. Specialized consideration towards IELTS and TOEFL test prep will be given if needed.
